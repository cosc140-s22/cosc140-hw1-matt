Excursion #1

Shortly after arriving at Cardiff, we traveled to Tintern Abbey for a fun excursion and exploration of Wales history.

From Tintern Abbey we traveled to Chepstow Castle to explore the innards of a REAL CASTLE! I bought a handy dandy wooden sword and dagger, and two awesome mini-metal knights.

After having a blast at the Castle, we decided to visit somewhere a bit more relaxing. We went on a tour of the Caerleon Roman Bath museum and got a snapshot of what life in the first three centuries was like for the average Joe.

Finally, we ended the trip with trip to the Caerleon outdoor Roman Amphitheater where we ran about some overgrown ruins and got distracted by some hairy cows.
